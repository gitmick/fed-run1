01What happened

Each participant ran a fresh, cold Claude session with only a CLAUDE.md brief and the two kton CLIs. Independently — no messaging between them — each wrote deterministic scripts, produced byte-identical outputs, signed a foton per step, committed the bytes with commit-pinned permalinks, and pushed. The aggregator polls each repo, fetches only what's new, verifies every Ed25519 signature, and aggregates by content hash. Because four independent runs produced the same bytes, every step lands at ↻4.

04What "verified" means here

Every record is content-addressed and signed. The viewer re-checks each Ed25519/DSSE signature in your browser against the published keys, and each foton carries a commit-pinned raw.githubusercontent.com/…/<sha>/… permalink — so you can fetch the exact input/output bytes and confirm they re-hash to the recorded value. Nothing asks you to trust the aggregator, or us.
